About Me

Hi there!
My name is Shanice, and I am currently an Object Oriented Software Development student at SAIT.
I will be finishing the program on October 2019.

My first experience with programming was during my undergrad in Electrical Engineering at The University of Calgary. The very first language I learned was C++. During my time in university, I also further practiced my programming skills with C, MIPS Assembly, VHDL. I also joined the university's Solar Car team where I get to play a little bit with the Arduino. For the last year of our undergrad, I was able to make our Capstone team succeed as Project Manager. We successfully built a wearable device that senses the user's muscle electric activity and increase their grip strength.

Now, as someone who has a natural aptitude for solving problems, and as someone who likes to constantly learn new things, I have shifted my interest to Software Development. I am finding everything that I am learning at SAIT fun and engaging. I have done multiple projects using the new skills I have learned, and have maintained excellent academic standing with a GPA of 4.0.
